ABOUT KATIE
Hi! Thanks For Stopping By!
I am passionate about smart design, user accessibility, and the opportunities that a modern website can bring to a small business owner.

While I always knew I wanted a career that allowed me creative expression, I'm also an empath and find deep satisifaction in helping others. I first studied Special Education and was a teacher for six years. I began experimenting with coding as a way to connect with my students, but after diving deeper, I decided to pursue web development full time. While I originally thought of my self-taught status as a weakness, I've since realized that my background gives me unique insight.

Need a website? We may be a good match if:
  • You'd rather hire one person instead of a team or agency. Websites are often built by a team of people, with the developer handling the code and and a web designer handling aesthetic decisions. A team may also have a graphic designer, who handles tasks like image optimization and logo design. I have skills in all of these areas.
  • You need a simple website. If you aren't sure what your needs are, I'm happy to discuss your business and website goals to determine how to best serve you!
  • You have an informal communication style. I have an artist brain - I don't gravitate towards schedules and protocols and prefer to keep client communication casual and convenient. It is a priority for me that you feel I am accessible and easy to collaborate with.
