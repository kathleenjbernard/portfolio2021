ABOUT KATIE
Hi! Thanks For Stopping By!
While I always knew I wanted a creative career, I don't feel fulfilled unless I'm helping others. I first studied Special Education and was a teacher for six years. I began writing code as a way to connect with my students, but as my interest grew, I began squeezing in time for my own projects when I wasn't writing lesson plans.

Deciding what to call myself has always been a challenge. I am passionate about design, user accessibility, and the opportunities that a strong online presence can bring a small business. I build websites, but I can also write content, give branding advice and design graphics.

Need a website? We may be a good match if:
  • You're on a budget. I'm self-taught and I've got cheap rates to match.
  • You'd rather work with just one person. Websites are often built by a team of people, with the developer handling the code and and a web designer handling aesthetic decisions. A team may also have a graphic designer, who handles tasks like image optimization and logo design.
  • Your website needs are simple. If you aren't sure what your needs are, I'm happy to discuss your business and website goals to determine how to best serve you!
  • You have an informal communication style. I have an artist's brain - I don't gravitate towards schedules and protocols and prefer to keep client communication casual and convenient. It is a priority for me that you feel I am accessible and easy to collaborate with.
